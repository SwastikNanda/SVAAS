1.   Our Main aim is to make a working prototype of Ventilater which will be of low cost and easily accessible for everyone. This ventilator will work in two modes namely Pressure Mode and Volume Mode using Arduino. Aim is to make this ventilator less dependent on Medical Equipments.
2.  Further, this project will switch its usage from Arduino to Raspberry Pi and also the screen attached with it will be enlarged by 7 inches and will be fully touch control. So, that it becomes easier to be operated by common man. Also, it will be interfaced with Android / IOS. So, that it can be handled via Mobile App. It will also go through rigorous testing on whether it working properly or not.
3.   Finalization of the Project.
Now, the ventilator is ready to get tested by medical associates and their team.
All the previous issues and bugs are fixed. And all the necessary procedures are to be followed.
